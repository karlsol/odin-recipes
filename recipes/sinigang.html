Sinigang

Description

Sinigang na Baboy is the ultimate comfort food! Made with pork ribs, vegetables, and tamarind-flavored broth, itâ€™s hearty and delicious on its own or served with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Fish sauce
  • Gabi
  • Radish
  • Sitaw
  • Tamarind
  • Banana or finger chili peppers (siling haba)

Steps

  1. Wash tamarind pods under cold, running water to remove any grit or dirt from the skins.
  2. Place in a saucepan with about 1 cup water and bring to a boil. Cook for about 4 to 5 minutes until soft and outer skins begin to burst.
  3. Using a fork, mash the tamarinds to release the pulp.
  4. In a fine-mesh strainer set over a bowl, place the tamarind and liquid. Continue to mash with a fork, returning some of the liquid into the strainer once or twice, to fully extract the juice.
  5. Discard seeds and skins. Pour tamarind juice into the pot.
